Travel Options

  • Over a dozen major cruise lines offer itineraries that feature destinations in India, between them presenting options ranging from one week in Southeast Asia to months-long voyages. Young adults would enjoy cruises as they get to visit different islands and territories of India. Major cruise lines offering cruises to India include Crystal, Costa, Cunard, P&O Cruises, Celebrity, and Holland America.

  • India is a popular flight destination and India receives over 68 million passengers every year. Young adults are encouraged to travel to India via flights as they are the fastest means of transport to India. There are many airlines that offer flights to India and some of them are Emirates, Qatar, Etihad, Air India, United, and Lufthansa.

Transportation

  • Traveling by train is one of Indiaâ€™s classic experiences. Young adults are encouraged to use trains as they are often on time and take them to their destination faster than any other transport other than flights. Young adults will get to experience unique trains and enjoyable journeys. The national rail network covers almost the entire country. There are three basic types of passenger trains in India: Inter-city trains, super-fast air-conditioned trains, and local passenger trains, which stop everywhere

  • Buses go almost everywhere, usually more frequently than trains. Young adults might prefer buses over trains as they are sometimes faster and easier to access than trains. Going by bus also usually saves you the bother of reserving a ticket in advance. Young adults who want to take short journeys around India at a quicker pace should travel using buses over trains.

Lodging

  • Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur is one of the largest residences of the royal family in India. Designed by renowned architect, Henry Lanchester, the hotel is the perfect blend of eastern and western architectural styles. Young adults will get to experience and live in the historical palace, once home to the royal family. Staying in Umaid Bhawan Palace is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, and is more like a journey through an era that redefined royalty and luxury

  • Taj Rambagh Palace, Jaipur is a famed luxury hotel that was once the residence of the Maharaja of Jaipur. Young adults will get to experience both the history behind the palace and get to live in a luxury hotel. The architecture of the hotel is a masterpiece and has great luxury decor and style. It justifies its royal looks by providing great hospitality, ensuring guests receive a class-apart experience.
